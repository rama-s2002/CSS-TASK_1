FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The 2014 FIFA World Cup was the 20th FIFA World Cup, the quadrennial world championship for men's national football teams organised by FIFA.

31 national teams advanced through qualification competitions to join the host nation in the final tournament (with Bosnia and Herzegovina)

It was the second time that Brazil staged the competition, the first being in 1950, and the fifth time that it was held in South America.
